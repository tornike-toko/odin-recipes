Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This tater tot casserole recipe is quick, easy, and incredibly satisfying. Made with simple and cheap ingredients, it's sure to please everyone at your dinner table.

ingredients
  • Beef
  • Canned soup
  • Seasonings
  • Tater tots
  • Cheese
Steps
  1. Cook the ground beef, then stir in the soup and seasonings.
  2. Transfer the beef to a baking dish. Top with tater tots, then the cheese.
  3. Bake until the tots are golden brown.
